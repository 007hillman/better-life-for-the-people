About better life for all

A Better Life For The people is a nonprofit and social association was created in 2019 in Douala in conformity with the provisions of law N° 90/053 of December 1990 on Freedom of association...
OBJECTIVES

Our objectives are pure and concise

We look to make life better for everyone because it's believed that with a better life, productivity and economic growth also follows

Health care facilities

We help improve health care facilities in rural communities so as to promote social welfare

Youth Vocations

We encourage the youths to take up vocations in health domain through seminars, conferences and workshops

Education

To promote education especially in health sector through capacity building and material support

Assist the needy

We assist the destitute by providing them with basic necessities such as clothes and food

Collaboration

We collaborate with associations with similar objectives for the improvement of the health of the needy, the down trodden and the poor
